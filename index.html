Who am I

My name is Alex, and I am studying to become a software developer. I moved to the USA on late 2019 by myself to go to college, however due to world events I had some issues achieving that goal. After taking a couple of years to get back on my feet I decided to sign up for a bootcamp as I concluded it would be the better option overall. Currently I am living in Maine.

About Me

Growing up I've always had an interest in creating things. Be it through art, writing, or coding, the idea of making something for others to perceive brings me joy. As a young teenager I discovered how to tamper with the files of games I enjoyed playing, however I didn't understand how moving some files around would cause such big changes in the games I would play. Seeing this would awaken the passion for coding that I have now. Web development is one of the many skills I will be learning to further my knowledge in Software Development. Since I moved to the USA I've worked a couple of jobs, the one I worked for the longest is a restaurant where I had to work with a team of waiters and cooks which I get along with really well. I am more than comfortable working in a team environment and am open to criticism, feedback, and co-operating with fellow co-workers.

My other main interest is art, I've drawn for as long as I have memory. I find joy in being able to recreate and leak my thoughts onto a sheet of paper so that I can share them with others.
